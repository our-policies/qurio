Introduction

Welcome to Qurio. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Information such as your name, email address, and phone number collected during registration or through your participation in our application's activities.
  • Activity Data: Data on your engagement with various games and activities like identifying colors, numbers, animals, words, birds, shapes, and sports; drawing animals; solving word puzzles; and performing arithmetic operations.
  • Device Information: Details about your device, including ID, model, manufacturer, and operating system version.
  • Financial Data: Limited financial information related to your transactions, with sensitive details managed by our payment processor.
  • Location Data: We may request access to your location to enhance application functionality, with options to adjust permissions in your device settings.
  • Push Notifications: Notifications for updates and relevant messages, with the ability to opt-out via device settings.

Your information helps us to:

  • Process transactions and provide related communication.
  • Offer customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Personalize user experience and enhance application features.
  • Conduct analytics to understand user behavior and improve our services.
  • Ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities.

  • Affiliates: We may share information with affiliates under similar privacy protections.
  • Business Partners: Information may be shared with partners for joint offerings and promotions.

Security of Your Information

We use various security measures to protect your personal information. Despite these efforts, please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Policy for Children

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove it promptly.
